How do I join the server?
To join, open Minecraft, go to "Multiplayer," click "Add Server," enter "nasi.lemak.xyz", and hit "Join Server."
What game modes are available?
There are various game modes available, such as survival, practice 1.8, practice 1.9, and anarchy. Each mode offers unique challenges and features.
Do I need to have a Premium account to join the server?
We aim to give everyone a chance to play on our Minecraft server, hence you can join using a cracked launcher, but we strongly recommend buying a premium account to support the game's development and for security purposes.
