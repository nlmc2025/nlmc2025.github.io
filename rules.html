Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Server rules

  1. Be respectful towards anyone, including players and staff members.
  2. Impersonating is not allowed, even if the victim was a player.
  3. Scamming in any way will be severely punished.
  4. Any racially discriminatory topics or words are not allowed.
  5. Spamming and flooding the chat are not allowed.
  6. No hate speech or offensive language towards any individual, group, or server.
  7. Main Chat is restricted to English and Malay only. Other languages are allowed only in private chats.
  8. Usernames and skins must not be offensive or inappropriate, such as those involving nudity or vulgarity.
  9. Discussions related to politics or other controversial subjects are not allowed to ensure a positive environment.
  10. You should know whether a topic is appropriate or inappropriate. Therefore, we do not tolerate, indecent talks/chats/acts, any NSFW Content or Self-Harm.
  11. For member's safety purpose, sharing anything related to Phishing links, IP Grabbers or suspicious links will result in an immediate permanent ban.
  12. Harassing and insulting are not allowed, please do not make other players to feel uncomfortable in any way, including but not limited to harassing in public and private chats, or in behavior. For players who are being harassed, you need to clearly express your refusal, otherwise no help will be provided.
  13. Excessive pinging of player or staff is prohibited, you do not need to ping the person multiple times as one ping would be enough to get their attention. In addition to this, If a player does excessive pinging, player may be punished such as getting Timed out in Discord or Muted in Game.
  14. No Advertising or Self-Promotion such as sharing links or IP addresses that promotes other servers, or posting referral content is prohibited. Mentions of names of other servers are acceptable, However promoting things like YouTube Links, Discord Servers, Minecraft IP's is not allowed.
  15. Stealing and Griefing are not allowed, even if the building/base is unclaimed or not fully claimed. However, if it's an unclaimed or not fully claimed generated structure, you are allowed to grief it. If you are not sure whether the building is generated or not, ask a staff member.
  16. You are allowed to claim a gameplay-related structures, such as End Portal, but you are not allowed to block the entrance and the portal in any way, including physically blocking it or using the NoEnter claim flag.
  17. Your claim must be 150 blocks away of other players' claim, even if you are expanding your claim. You can get the other player's consent to claim within 150 blocks of their claim through any method that does not violate the server rules.
  18. Hacking and Exploiting are not allowed, doing so will result in a warning, second violation will result in a permanent ban. If you have found a bug, please report it in Discord or tell any staff member, we might compensate you some rewards. Autoclicker/macro will also be considered as hacking.
  19. Selling in-game items for real-life money is strictly prohibited and both sellers and buyers will be permanently banned. However, you are allowed to sell anything in the server store for any in-game items including balance.
  20. Staff are not allowed to provide any help to players in dm. If there is such a situation, you can report it to the higher management.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
